About the Project

Preserving the Sindhi Language
Through Modern AI

Sindhi is one of the oldest languages in South Asia, spoken by over 25 million people worldwide. This tool bridges the gap between Romanized Sindhi — commonly used in digital communication — and the traditional Perso-Arabic script that preserves the cultural heritage of the Sindhi people.

Dual AI Models

Two separate models run in parallel — mBART and mT5 — giving you two transliteration perspectives for comparison and accuracy.

Personal Accounts

Each user has a private account. Your history is yours alone — no other user can see your conversions or inputs.

History Tracking

Every conversion is saved to your personal history. Revisit past transliterations and reload them with a single click.

Fast & Local

Models run locally on your machine — no internet dependency for inference, ensuring speed and complete data privacy.

Technology

Two Models, One Goal

Compare outputs from two cutting-edge sequence-to-sequence models fine-tuned for Roman to Perso-Arabic Sindhi transliteration.

mBART

Multilingual BART

A large pre-trained sequence-to-sequence model supporting 25 languages. Fine-tuned on Sindhi parallel data for high-fidelity transliteration with strong contextual understanding.

mT5

Multilingual T5

Google's multilingual text-to-text transformer covering 101 languages. Fine-tuned specifically for Sindhi script conversion with excellent morphological accuracy.
